Every Day Gym Schedule

Every 3 weeks add extra set

  • On Sunday's take as rest day, include a good amount of protein with high calories.
  • On Monday's you hit all upper body (Back/Shoulder/Ab's). Include ' Bent-Over Rows 3x15, Lat Pulldown's 2x10, One Arm Bent Over Row's 2x10, Kneeling Lat Extension's 3xFailure, Shoulder Press 3x10, Standing Lateral Raises 3x10, Reverse Fly's 3x10, 1min Low Plank, and Laying Ab Crunches 3x20 '
  • On Tuesday's go to ' Change of Direction, Speed & Agility training '
  • On Wednesday's take as rest day, include a good amount of protein with high calories.
  • On Thursday's you hit all lower body (Hamstring/Quads/Glutes). Include ' Squats 3x8, Goblet Squats 3x15, Hamstring Curls 2x15, Standing Leg Extensions 3x10, Romanian Deadlifts 2x8, and Reverse Lunges 2x8 '
  • On Friday's you hit all upper body (Chest/Tricep/Bicep). Include ' Chest Press 3x15, Chest Flys 2x10, Push-Ups 2x20, Overhead Tricep Extensions 3x10, Concentrated Bicep Curls 3xFailure, and Kneeling Tricep Extensions 2x15 '
  • On Saturday's do Straight Line Speed. Include ' 10yd Sprints 4x5, Hill Sprints 4x5, 10yd Sled Sprints 2x3, Flying 10's, and 1+ Mile Jog '
